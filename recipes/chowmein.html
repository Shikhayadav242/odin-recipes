Chow Mein Recipe


Ingredients


  • 200g noodles
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, cabbage)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

STEPS


  1. Prep the noodles:
    • Cook the noodles according to package instructions.
    • Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the pan
    • Use a wok or wide pan.
    • Heat oil on high flame until hot.
  3. Sauté aromatics
    • Add chopped garlic and ginger.
    • Stir-fry for a few seconds until fragrant.
  4. Cook the veggies
    • Add mixed vegetables.
    • Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until tender.
  5. Add sauces & seasoning
    • Stir in soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce, and ketchup. Mix well.
    • Sprinkle salt and black pepper.
  6. Mix noodles
    • Add boiled noodles to the pan.
    • Toss everything on high flame for 2 to 3 minutes so noodles absorb the sauces.
  7. Garnish & serve
    • Add spring onion greens.
    • Serve hot with chili sauce or Manchurian.

TIPS!

  • Do not overcook noodles—they should be slightly firm (al dente).
  • Stir-fry on high flame to get that smoky street-style flavor.
  • You can also add scrambled egg, chicken, or prawns for a non-veg version.
