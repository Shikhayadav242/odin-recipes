Aalu Tikki Recipe


Ingredients


  • 200g boiled potatoes, mashed
  • 1/2 cup green peas, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons fresh coriander, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for shallow frying

STEPS


  1. Prep the potatoes
    • Boil the potatoes until tender.
    • Peel and mash them in a bowl.
  2. Mix spices and binding
    • Add bread crumbs, green chili, ginger, coriander, red chili powder, cumin, garam masala, and salt.
    • Sprinkle cornflour and mix everything well to form a dough-like mixture.
  3. Shape the tikkis
    • Take a portion of the mixture and shape it into a small patty.
    • Repeat with the remaining mixture.
  4. Heat the pan and cook tikkis
    • Add oil for shallow frying.
    • Once hot, add the shaped tikkis.
    • Fry until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Garnish & serve
    • Place on tissue to remove extra oil.
    • Serve hot with green chutney, tamarind chutney, or ketchup.

TIPS!

  • For extra crispiness, coat the tikkis lightly in bread crumbs before frying.
  • You can also stuff them with green peas or paneer for a filling version.
